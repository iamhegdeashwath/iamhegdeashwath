Project: Complaints Tracking System
Role: Software Engineer (Intern)
Description:

This is an application designed to lodge complaints over their intranet. Any employee working in any department can lodge complaint regarding their machinery, software, hardware, network, and also general complaints like insufficient stationary. This enables all the department heads to keep an eye on the complaints that are lodged by their employees. Whenever a complaint is opened by the vendor or stores department the open time and dated are stored in the database, thereby by enabling the system to calculate the turnaround time of the solution and can close when resolved.
